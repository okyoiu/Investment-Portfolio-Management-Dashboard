# ✅ Subscription Management Feature Added!

## 🎨 What Was Added

### 1. **Subscription Manager Component**
- Shows all active subscriptions for selected account
- Add, edit, and cancel subscriptions
- Tracks billing dates and amounts
- Categorizes subscriptions (Entertainment, Software, Health, etc.)

### 2. **Smart Suggestions**
- **High Subscription Ratio** - Warns if subscriptions exceed 10% of balance
- **Duplicate Categories** - Alerts if you have 3+ subscriptions in same category
- **Upcoming Renewals** - Shows subscriptions renewing in next 7 days
- **Positive Reinforcement** - Praises good subscription management

### 3. **Account Insights Component**
- Contextual tips based on selected account
- Balance alerts (negative, low, healthy)
- Account-type specific advice
- Spending ratio analysis

### 4. **Quick Tips Component**
- Rotating financial tips
- 6 different tips covering various topics
- Interactive navigation (prev/next buttons)
- Fills empty spaces with useful content

## 🎯 How It Works

### When You Click an Account:
1. **Selected Account Info** appears at top
2. **Subscription Manager** shows below (with all subscriptions)
3. **Account Insights** appear in sidebar
4. All components update based on selected account
